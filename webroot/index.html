Reimagining Democracy

We believe our Democracy is a social technology and like most technologies improving our experience through innovation is a good thing. OurSociety makes politics as easy or in-depth as you would like it to be. We can match you to the best candidates running for office based on your personal values and you can read about candidates policies, plans, and visions to develop your own perspective. Information is vetted to ensure the factual content and remove the partisanship.

Expanding Possibilities

OurSociety is non-profit, non-partisan online platform designed for political candidates and existing politicians that enables them to both run their political campaigns and connect with their constituents on a year round basis. We’re extending the reach of qualified individuals and removing the reliance on corporate donations to run a political campaign. OurSociety is breaking the barriers associated with running for office and empowering a new generation of leaders who don’t fit within the current mold of thinking.

Creating Collaboration

Part of OurSociety’s purpose is to help shift the culture U.S. politics from divisive showmanship based around a few wedge issues to a more collaborative approach among the citizens. Using the data we collect from individual value profiles we will identify key desires among the voting populace that we all agree should be addressed. This information will be made available to the public, empowering both citizens and political candidates to work towards our unified common goals. We believe we have more in common than we give ourselves credit for and this approach is the first step of many towards a better system of being.
